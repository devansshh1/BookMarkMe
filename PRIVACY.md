# Privacy Policy for BookMarkMe

**Effective Date:** May 12, 2026

BookMarkMe ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding the BookMarkMe Chrome Extension.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
BookMarkMe is designed with a **zero-server architecture**. 
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
- **Local Storage:** Any bookmarks you create, including snippets of chat text and conversation URLs, are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. 

### 2. Data Transmission
- **No External Transmission:** We do not transmit your chat data, prompts, or bookmarks to any external servers.
- **Third-Party Services:** We do not share your data with third-party services, advertisers, or data brokers.

### 3. Permissions
- **Host Permissions (chatgpt.com):** Used strictly to identify message elements for bookmarking and to provide automated navigation (scrolling) within the chat interface.
- **Storage:** Used to save your bookmarks locally so they persist across browser sessions.

### 4. Security
Your bookmarks are as secure as your local browser profile. We recommend following standard security practices for your browser and device.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" above.

### 6. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/devansshh1/BookMarkMe).
